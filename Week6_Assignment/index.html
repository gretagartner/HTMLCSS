Vegan Pasta Salad

Ingredients

Rotini Noodles-16oz
Cucumber-1 small diced
Red Onion- 1/2 diced
Vegan Mayo-1 Cup (I use Hellman's Plant-based)
Apple Cider Vinegar-2 TBSP
Italian Seasoning-1 TSP
Garlic Powder-1/2 TSP
Salt, Pepper-Small pinch for taste
Chickpeas-1 can (drained)
Red Bell Pepper-1/2 Diced
Black Olives-1/2 can diced (drained)
Pickles-7 diced (small)
Pickle Juice-2 TBSP

Directions

Bring water to a boil and put noodles in. Cook for 8-10 minutes or until tender.
Drain pasta and let cool.
Combine Mayo, apple cider vinegar, italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt, pepper and pickle juice into a large bowl.
Dice the cucumber, red onion, red bell pepper, black olives and pickle and put in bowl. At this time, the chickpeas can be drained, rinsed and added. Mix all ingredients together.
Add pasta into bowl, mix well, and chill in fridge for an hour.
You are done!
